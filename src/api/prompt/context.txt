The Crew Training Database manages aircraft crew training, certifications, and logistics, ensuring compliance and readiness. It tracks qualifications, training events, and related data.

Key Tables:

employees: Employee details, rank, and location.
ranks: Hierarchy of positions (e.g., Captain, First Officer).
qualifications: Certifications (e.g., ATPL License, Type Ratings).
rank_qualifications: Links ranks to required qualifications.
trainer_licenses: Tracks instructor certifications.
locations: Training/work locations with geographic data.
courses: Training programs (duration, attendees, prerequisites).
course_events: Actual training events (schedule, attendees).
